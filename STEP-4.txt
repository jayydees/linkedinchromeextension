SUBMISSION PROCESS:
-------------------

1. Go to Chrome Web Store Developer Dashboard:
   https://chrome.google.com/webstore/devconsole

2. Sign in with your Google account

3. If first time: Pay $5 developer registration fee

4. Click "New Item" button

5. Upload your ZIP file (from Step 3)

6. Click "Continue"

7. Fill in the Store Listing:

   Extension Name:
   LinkedIn Saved Posts Organizer

   Summary (copy this):
   Organize, label, search, and manage your LinkedIn saved posts with custom tags, pins, and notes.

   Description:
   (Open the file: CHROME_STORE_SUBMISSION.md and copy the "Detailed Description" section)

   Category:
   Productivity

   Language:
   English

8. Upload Screenshots:
   - Take 3-5 screenshots of the extension working on LinkedIn
   - Recommended size: 1280x800 pixels
   - Show: the panel, labels, pins, search features

9. Privacy Settings:

   Privacy Policy URL:
   https://github.com/jayydees/linkedinchromeextension/blob/main/PRIVACY.md

   (Or host PRIVACY.md on your own website and use that URL)

   Does it handle personal data? YES
   What data? User activity, Personally identifiable information
   How handled? Processed and stored locally (NOT transmitted)
   ‚úÖ Check: "Does not sell user data"

10. Distribution:
    - Visibility: Public
    - Regions: All regions
    - Pricing: Free

11. Review everything carefully

12. Click "Submit for Review"

13. Wait for approval (usually 1-3 days, can take up to 7 days)

14. You'll get an email when it's approved!
